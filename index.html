About Me

Hello! I am a highly motivated UX designer with a love for color, an eye for detail, and a passion for innovative design and sustainability.

My previous design work was in fashion design, specializing in Children's wear, but if you want to go even earlier than that, it started when I was just 5-years-old, when my mom taught me how to sew. Little did I know when I made my very first pair of pajama pants that I could turn my love for creating into an amazing career.

With my skills in research, user empathy and accessible design, I create accessible designs that are impactful, that will weaves together usability and delight.
